Welcome to Đardin

Located right on the beautiful coast of Kaštel Stari, Beach bar Đardin offers a peaceful escape with breathtaking views of the Adriatic Sea.

Whether you're sipping your morning macchiato under the pine trees or enjoying a refreshing cocktail as the sun goes down, our friendly staff is here to make your day perfect.
