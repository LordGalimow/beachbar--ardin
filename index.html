Welcome to Đardin

Nestled directly on the pristine coast of Kaštel Stari, Beach bar Đardin offers an elegant retreat framed by panoramic views of the Adriatic Sea.

Whether you are enjoying an exquisite morning espresso beneath the coastal pines or sipping a handcrafted sunset cocktail, our dedicated team ensures an unforgettable experience.
